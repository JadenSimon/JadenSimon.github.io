Political Tweet Analysis

Team Members: Brandon Mouser, Kyle Price, and Jaden Simon

Project Summary

Political tweet analysis was a data mining project that aimed to analyze tweets and Russian advertisements from the 2016 Presidential election. The goal was to see if the advertisements were emulating what the candidates were tweeting in order to influence the outcome of the election.

Data was gathered from a public repository and all analysis was performed using Python. A few key analysis techniques were ad/tweet similarity, word-embedding, topic extraction, and sentiment analysis. A word-cloud and corpus characteristic chart was also made to visually see the differences in datasets.

Our final results were inconclusive as the similarity between tweets and ads were not greater than what they would be through random chance. Further analysis techniques could show otherwise.
